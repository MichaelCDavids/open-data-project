What We Do

Our aim is to make a difference in helping you know more about Street People. The focus is to make statistics easily accessible to the public. There are various social issues that affect everyone in South Africa, which is why we have decided to address the issue of people living on our streets. Homeless people are left on the fringes of society, as if they do not belong. We believe that what we do can and will be used to empower the homeless and help to visualise the plight of NGO’s and shelters trying to make a difference.
