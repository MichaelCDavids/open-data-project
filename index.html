About

With this website we aim to make s difference in helping people know more about Street People(Reasing Awareness).The focus is to make statistics accesable for the public. There are various social issues that affect everyone in South Africa, and that is why we have decided to create awareness for homeless people. Homeless people are left on the fringes of society, as if not belonging to society. We believe that our website will create and be used to uplight and display the plight of NGO’s and shelters trying to make a difference. From the data that we have collected, we can see that there is a lack of shelters in Cape Town to cater for homeless people.
